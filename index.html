Empowering AI researchers to share fully reproducible and portable model implementations.

Crowdsourced through contributions by the scientific research community, modelhub is a repository of self-contained deep learning models pretrained for a wide variety of applications. Modelhub highlights recent trends in deep learning applications, enables transfer learning approaches, and promotes reproducible science.
